I had the chance to visit Ooty, the “Queen of Hill Stations,” and it was an unforgettable experience. As soon as I reached, the cool breeze and misty hills welcomed me with open arms. The journey itself was mesmerizing, with winding roads, lush green valleys, and breathtaking views all around. One of the highlights was visiting the Ooty Botanical Garden, where I saw a wide variety of flowers and plants. The Ooty Lake was equally beautiful—I enjoyed a peaceful boat ride there. Walking through the tea plantations was another magical moment; the smell of fresh tea leaves and the scenic beauty made me feel refreshed and calm..
